Astronomy Equipment

A guide to the essential tools used to observe and photograph the deep sky.

🔭

Observe the Sky

To observe the sky effectively, find a dark, open location away from city lights and allow 20 minutes for your eyes to adapt to the dark. Use your naked eye, binoculars, or a telescope to identify constellations, planets—which do not twinkle—and the Milky Way, ideally using apps like Stellarium to guide you..

Telescopes

Telescopes are optical instruments that collect and concentrate light using mirrors or lenses to magnify distant objects. They enable astronomers to view faint, far-off objects by gathering light, with larger apertures offering better resolution. Common types include refractors (lenses), reflectors (mirrors), and compound systems

Astrophotography

Astrography primarily refers to the scientific mapping of the heavens, but today it is most commonly associated with Astrography.com, a leading global studio that specializes in space-themed art and professional astrophotography prints.
